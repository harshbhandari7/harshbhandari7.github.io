Selected work

Latest · Applied AI

Doc-RAG Engine

A production-grade RAG pipeline built to answer a question that actually matters: does chunking strategy change retrieval quality? It indexes documents into Qdrant with dense and sparse vectors, fuses results with reciprocal rank fusion, reranks with a cross-encoder, and generates grounded answers through any OpenAI-compatible LLM. Every configuration is measured, not guessed: a 12-run RAGAS evaluation matrix tracked in MLflow, explored through a Streamlit dashboard.
